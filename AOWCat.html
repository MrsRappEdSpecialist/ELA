Article of the Week: Anaheim Through the Lens

By Javier Uribe and Jonathon F. Negrete | Published February 23, 2026

Standards: RI.9-10.1 (Evidence), RI.9-10.2 (Central Idea), W.9-10.4 (Clear Writing)

UDL Vocabulary & Support

Key Terms

Nostalgia: A sentimental longing for the past.

Grueling: Extremely tiring and demanding.

Photography Term

Lens: The part of a camera that focuses light; also a way of "seeing" the world.

T4: Talk to the Text (Annotations)

1. Anaheim Through the Lens is a series to pay attention to details of this high school. From the different great styles to the beautiful new football field. Even to the cute orange cat that walks around our campus.

2. Our campus is full of amazing and exciting details most of us never notice. So we walked with our cameras to notice them. In this article I will also go over what it means to be a photographer at Anaheim High School.

3. The beautiful details are what make this school special. A very famous and cute detail is the adorable orange cat that roams our campus daily, walking around from one area to another. Even relaxing at tables with fellow students. The cat gets too comfortable sometimes he even falls asleep at the lunch tables.

4. A new football and track field is a new start to the careers of the future generations. The future generation will remember running this new state of the art track. Our future football players will remember their grueling practice. This is a place where we create nostalgia and memories.

5. In conclusion, what it means to be a photographer at Anaheim High School is to look around from the unexpected cats to the expected football field bringing us happiness. To see amazing outfits or the first thing people saw when they woke up. Every part of this school has a story; we just have to look deeper.
